What is it?

Big data analytics is a disruptive technology that can reshape core tasks of security intelligence. The real-time discovery of hidden criminal patterns is an outstanding challenge for security and law enforcement agencies. In particular, predicting the evolution of criminal networks and uncovering concealed relationships can efficiently guide investigations for better decision-making. In this context, it is necessary to develop social network metrics that are both domainaware and ready to be executed in a data stream environment. That is why we propose two metrics for link detection and prediction, together with their data stream processing implementation.
